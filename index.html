Our Story

Born from a
Late-Night Panic

It started when our founder, Nkechi, stayed up until 2am trying to plan a surprise for her mum — calling vendors, sourcing balloons, bribing a hotel manager. It was chaos. But the look on her mum's face the next morning? Worth everything.

That moment sparked an idea: nobody should have to figure this out alone. SurpriseBox was born to take the stress, guesswork, and logistics off your hands — so you can focus entirely on the person you love.

Today we're a passionate team of event stylists, decorators, and delivery specialists based in Lagos — making magic for hundreds of Nigerians every year. 🇳🇬
